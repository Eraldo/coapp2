Community Guidelines I have accepted the following guidelines: Stop Rule

When someone says ‘Stop’ it means ‘Stop’! I respect that and disengage for a while.

Discretion

I treat the privacy of other members with respect. If I want to share with non-members, I focus on sharing my feelings and thoughts and avoid associating names.

Self-Responsibility

I am responsible for my own experience.

 Appreciation

I am willing to treat the club and its content and members with appreciation.

 Feedback

If I do not like something, I actively provide feedback by sharing my insecurities instead of resorting to blame or passive agression.

 Structure

I accept the general structure and terms of the project. If in doubt I seek to understand and learn more about its context and perspective.

 Purpose

I proactively try to understand and act in alignment with the purpose of things even if there are not explicit rules for them.
